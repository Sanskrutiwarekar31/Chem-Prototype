Questions & Answers

What is soap?

Soap is a cleaning agent made by reacting oils with alkali.

Why is sodium hydroxide used?

It helps in the chemical reaction called saponification.

Is handmade soap safe?

Yes, if prepared correctly with proper safety measures.
